Background

Indonesia Re-Launch of A Social Media Mobile App

Migme is a global digital media company focused on emerging markets. Migme was formed in 2006. In 2014 it was rebranded from Mig33 to Migme which included a product and corporate overhaul. The migme mobile app is a mobile entertainment platform that connects celebrities and influencers with their fans and has more than 30 million active users.
