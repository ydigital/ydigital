our people

With only 45 people, we are not biggest team, but we pick quality over quantity. Time and again we outperform bigger agencies with shiny client lists and fancy titles. Our secret lies in our people strategy; we put our people first and treat them well. We do so with a belief that they will do the same for our clients.

boye hartman
/ board of director

Boye Hartmann

Board of Director

Boye is an online and mobile marketing expert with a big entrepreneurial heart. He has succesfully started companies around the world and also held executive positions in global companies within business development, branding and marketing. Boye frequently speaks at conferences and events and is also an advisor at Founders Institute and Copenhagen Business School.

peter haarmark
/ group ceo

peter haarmark

Group CEO

With experience from Europe, USA and Asia, Peter leads our talented multinational team. Before moving to Asia, Peter co-founded Idomove, a fitness wearables and gamification company that quickly reached 80,000 organic app downloads and users in more than 100 countries. Inspired by the digital opportunity in Asia, Peter moved to Indonesia where he co-founded Ydigital Asia as COO. In 2016, he established our Malaysia presence and today he serves as our group CEO focusing on growing our team, leadership and services.

fredrik johansson

fredrik johansson

Directur, Consulting

Before joining Ydigital, Fredrik worked for Deloitte and Capgemini as a management consultant where he worked with some of the largest companies with change management and digital transformation. Fredrik leads our consulting team and his structured mindset help clients establish simplicity where there used to be chaos.

COO

Yudha is a living example that a positive mindset and hard work can take you anywhere. Yudha joined Ydigital when we were just 6 months old as an Account Manager.
